Polyglot E-Commerce Project - Possible Interview Questions

1. What is polyglot persistence and why did you use it in this project?
2. How does microservices architecture benefit an e-commerce system?
3. How did you implement communication between services?
4. Why did you choose PostgreSQL for users and orders, and MongoDB for products?
5. How does JWT authentication work in your system?
6. What happens if the product-service is down when placing an order?
7. How do you ensure data consistency across microservices?
8. What are the advantages and disadvantages of REST communication in microservices?
9. How would you scale this system?
10. If given more time, what improvements would you make (e.g., Redis, Docker, unit tests)?
